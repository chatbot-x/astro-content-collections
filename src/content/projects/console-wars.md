## About the Series

**Console Wars** chronicles the history of video games through the lens of the corporate rivalries that defined each generation. From Atari's collapse to Nintendo's resurrection, from Sega's rebellion to Sony's dominance, from Microsoft's entry to the mobile revolution — every chapter is a story of ambition, creativity, and the relentless pursuit of the living room.
